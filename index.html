Questions

What kind of tubes do you accept?

Any brand, any size. Travel tubes, family-sized tubes, whatever. The more variety the better. Just make sure it's actually empty.

Where will they be displayed?

Somewhere in Charleston, West Virginia. Once we've collected and filled all the tubes with plaster, they will be displayed.

Can I write something on my tube?

Yes. Grab a Sharpie and write your name, a date, a message, a drawing — whatever you want. It becomes part of the collection.

Does it need the cap?

Caps are good. If it still has the cap, leave it on.

Is this serious?

Yes, it is very serious.
