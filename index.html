Questions

What kind of tubes do you accept?

Any brand, any size. Travel tubes, family-sized tubes, whatever. The more variety the better. Just make sure it's actually empty.

Does it need the cap?

Caps are good. If it still has the cap, leave it on.

Will the tubes be displayed?

Yes. Once we've collected and filled all the tubes with plaster, the final masterpiece will be displayed somewhere in Charleston, West Virginia.

Can I write something on my tube?

Yes. Grab a Sharpie and write your name, a date, a message, a drawing...whatever you want. It's your tube.
