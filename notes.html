Thoughts, updates, questions, and random stuff about collecting empty toothpaste tubes.

March 25, 2026
collect

How many could you actually collect?

Charleston has about 47,000 people. If the average person goes through maybe 4 tubes a year, that's 188,000 tubes floating around this city annually. Most of those are going straight into the garbage. Even if I got 1% of them that's almost 2,000 tubes.

March 25, 2026
fill

How would you fill them all?

One tube at a time is fine when you have six. But what about sixty? Or six hundred? Need to figure out a system. Maybe a funnel rig. Maybe a big batch of plaster and an assembly line. This is going to get messy.

March 25, 2026
display

How would you display them?

A big pile would be the simplest but feels lazy. A wall mount could look cool — rows and rows of tubes like a mosaic. Or maybe some kind of structure, like building blocks. What if you could build a chair out of them? A wall? Depends how many we end up with.
